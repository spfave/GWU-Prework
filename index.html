Bio

Amy Noonan is an an American rapper, singer, and songwriter performing under the stage name Qveen Herby. Amy started in the music industry as part of the music duo Karmin with partner Nick Noonan. Together they released two studio albums under Epic records as Karmin before taking a break and coming back as an independent mucic group with Amy as the lead persona and the Qveen Herby rebrand while Nick took on a bigger role on the production side. Since the rebrand in 2017 Qveen herby has released nine EP records with three charting in the Billboard charts in the US. In addition to her music Qveen Herby has also launched her own branded merchandise and makeup available on Qveen Studios.
